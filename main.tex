\section*{Problem 12.4.1}

Show that, for $|s\rangle = \cos(\theta / 2) | \uparrow \rangle +
\exp(i\phi)\sin(\theta / 2) | \downarrow \rangle$,

$
\langle s |\hat{\boldsymbol{\sigma}} | s \rangle =
\bm{i}\sin\theta\cos\phi +
\bm{j}\sin\theta\sin\phi +
\bm{k}\cos\theta
$.

$|s\rangle = (1/\sqrt2)
[|\uparrow\rangle + i| \uparrow \rangle]
$, and that the electron spin magnetic moment operator can be written as
$\hat{\boldsymbol{\mu}}_e = g \mu_B\hat{\boldsymbol{\sigma}}$.
What is the expectation value of the electron spin magnetic moment?
